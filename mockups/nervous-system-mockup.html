The Sway Test

Learn how your body communicates YES and NO

The Sway Test is simple but powerful.

Stand up straight with your feet hip-width apart. Close your eyes if it helps you tune in.

Say out loud: "Show me a YES." Notice which way your body sways‚Äîforward, back, left, or right.

Say out loud: "Show me a NO." Notice the contrast.

There's no right direction. Your body has its own language. Let it show you.

If you're skeptical, that's okay. Just try it with curiosity.
