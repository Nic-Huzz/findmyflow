How to Read the Color Coding

Each answer is color-coded to help you identify the most desirable customer persona for your offer. The colors represent:

White = Least desirable
Yellow = Moderately desirable
Orange = Very desirable
Red = Most desirable

What Makes a Persona Desirable?

Pain Level (1-10):

Higher pain = More motivated to buy. A customer at 10/10 pain is desperate for a solution.

Problem Relates To:

Wealth problems = Highest willingness to invest
Health problems = Moderate willingness
Life satisfaction = Lower urgency

Income Level:

Higher income = Greater purchasing power and ability to invest in solutions.

Financial Sunk Cost:

None/Minimal = No switching costs, easier to sell
Substantial = Already invested elsewhere, harder to switch

Time Sunk Cost:

None = Haven't wasted time on failed solutions, more open
Substantial (2+ years) = May be skeptical or stuck

Current Emotion:

Any emotion listed = Shows awareness and urgency
None of the above = Less emotional engagement

Pro Tip

Look for personas with more orange and red answers. These represent customers who are in pain, have money, and haven't already invested heavily in other solutions.
